Neem Tree 🌿

All About the Amazing Neem Tree! 🌳

Introduction to Neem 🌱

The neem tree, scientifically known as Azadirachta indica, is a powerful medicinal plant that has been used for centuries for its health benefits. It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is often called the "village pharmacy" because of its wide range of uses. 💊🌿

Interesting Facts About Neem 🌱

  • Neem is known for its natural insecticidal properties! 🦠
  • The tree's leaves, bark, and seeds are used in traditional medicine. 🌿💊
  • Neem oil is a great natural remedy for skin conditions like acne. 🧴🌿
  • Neem tree helps to purify the air and acts as a natural air filter! 🌬️🍃
  • The neem tree can grow up to 15-20 meters tall! 🌳

Neem Trees in IIT Bombay 🌳

Did you know that IIT Bombay has a rich diversity of trees? 🌳 As part of the ongoing research and sustainability projects, IITB has over 15,000 trees 🌲🌳, with a significant number of them being Neem trees!

Out of these, there are around 500 Neem trees 🌿, contributing to the environmental health and greenery on the campus. The Neem tree is loved for its medicinal properties and environmental benefits! 💚
