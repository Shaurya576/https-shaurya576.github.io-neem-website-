Neem Tree (Azadirachta Indica) 🌿

The neem tree is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent. Known for its numerous health benefits, neem is often referred to as the "village pharmacy" 🌍. It's also known for its medicinal properties 🌱.

🌟 Interesting Facts:

  • 🌿 Neem leaves have natural antibacterial properties ✨.
  • 🌳 The tree can grow up to 20 meters in height 🏞️.
  • 🧴 Neem oil is used in many cosmetics and skincare products 💆‍♀️.
  • 💊 It is widely used in traditional medicine in India and other parts of Asia 🌍.

🔬 Scientific Classification:

  • Scientific Name: Azadirachta Indica 🧬
  • Family: Meliaceae 🌳
  • Common Names: Neem, Indian Lilac 🌿

🌸 Characteristics:

  • 🍃 Leaves: Dark green, pinnate leaves with a strong aroma 🌱.
  • 🌸 Flowers: Small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in panicles 🌼.
  • 🌳 Fruit: Small, yellow-green fruits containing seeds 🌰.
