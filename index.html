Neem Tree - Azadirachta Indica 🌳

What is a Neem Tree? 🌿

The Neem tree (Azadirachta indica) is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent. It's known for its numerous medicinal and ecological benefits. 🌱 It's also often referred to as the "Miracle Tree" because of its various uses in treating ailments, purifying the air, and promoting overall health. 💚

Neem leaves are used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral properties. 🌿 Its seeds, bark, and oil are also widely used for natural remedies. 🌼

Neem Trees in IIT Bombay 🌳

Did you know that IIT Bombay has a rich diversity of trees? 🌳 As part of the ongoing research and sustainability projects, IITB has over 15,000 trees 🌲🌳, with a significant number of them being Neem trees!

Out of these, there are around 500 Neem trees 🌿, contributing to the environmental health and greenery on the campus. The Neem tree is loved for its medicinal properties and environmental benefits! 💚

Interesting Facts about Neem Trees 🧐

  • Neem trees can grow up to 15-20 meters tall! 🌳
  • The Neem tree is drought-resistant and can survive in harsh conditions. 💧
  • The leaves, flowers, and seeds of the Neem tree have been used in Ayurvedic medicine for thousands of years. 🌿
  • Neem trees are used to purify the air and improve the environment. 🌍
  • Neem oil is a natural pesticide, used in organic farming to protect crops! 🌱

Neem Tree Characteristics 🌿

  • Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica 🌿
  • Common Name: Neem Tree, Indian Lilac 🌳
  • Family: Meliaceae 🌱
  • Height: 15-20 meters 🌳
  • Leaves: Pinnate, aromatic 🌿
  • Flowers: Small, white, fragrant 🌸
