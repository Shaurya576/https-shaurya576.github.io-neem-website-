🌿 About the Neem Tree (Azadirachta indica)

The neem tree is a fast-growing, evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent. It’s widely known for its medicinal, antibacterial, and insect-repelling properties. 🌱

Hindi Name: नीम

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

🌳 Neem Trees in IIT Bombay

There are approximately 4,000 trees in IIT Bombay. Out of them, nearly 250 are Neem trees! 🌳🌿

✨ Interesting Facts About Neem Tree

  • 🌿 Neem is called the "Village Pharmacy" in India.
  • 🦟 It naturally repels mosquitoes and pests.
  • 🧴 Used in skincare and beauty products worldwide.
  • 🌱 Helps purify the air and improve soil fertility.
  • 🍃 Neem oil is used in natural agriculture as a pesticide.
  • 🌳 It can live for over 200 years!
  • 💚 Neem leaves are used to treat skin disorders.
  • 🌞 Offers excellent shade in hot climates.
  • 🛡️ Has antifungal, antiviral, and antibacterial properties.
  • 🧘 Used in Ayurvedic treatments for centuries.
  • 🐛 Neem oil is eco-friendly and non-toxic to animals.
  • 🌿 Its bark is used in toothpaste for healthy gums.
  • 🍀 Neem twigs are used as natural toothbrushes.
  • 💪 Boosts immunity and helps fight infections.
  • 🌬️ It improves overall air quality around it.
  • ☀️ Grows well in dry and hot climates.
  • 🌏 Popular in countries like India, Africa, and the Middle East.
  • 🌼 Neem flowers are small and fragrant.
  • 🌰 Neem seeds are used for oil extraction.
  • 🧠 Known for enhancing memory in Ayurveda.
