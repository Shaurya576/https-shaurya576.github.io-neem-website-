About the Neem Tree 🌳

The Neem tree (Azadirachta indica) is known for its medicinal properties 💊🌱. It is often referred to as the "village pharmacy" 🏥💚.

Features & Benefits of the Neem Tree 🌿

  • Natural pesticide 🐜🛑
  • Anti-inflammatory properties 🔥🤕
  • Boosts immune system 💪💚
  • Improves skin health 🌟🧴
  • Eco-friendly 🌍💚

Neem Trees at IIT Bombay 🌳🎓

There are around 200 Neem trees at IIT Bombay 🌳📚. They provide shade and contribute to a greener campus 🌿🌞.
