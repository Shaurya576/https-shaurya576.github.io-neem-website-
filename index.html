📛 Common Name: Neem

🌟 Interesting Facts about Neem 🌟💫

  • 📜 Neem has been used for over 2000 years in India 🇮🇳
  • 🦟 Acts as a natural mosquito repellent 💪
  • 💅 Neem oil is used in cosmetics and skin care
  • 🛡️ Called the "Village Pharmacy" for its healing powers
  • 🌾 Used by farmers as a natural pesticide
  • 🍃 Leaves help purify the air & environment
  • 🪥 Twigs used as natural toothbrushes (datun)
  • 💊 Used in Ayurveda and Unani medicine systems
  • 🧪 Rich in antioxidants and antifungal properties
  • 🌍 Helps promote sustainable agriculture
  • 💧 Survives in dry areas with less water
  • 👶 Safe for use in baby products
  • 😷 Helps treat skin infections & rashes
  • 🧼 Common in herbal soaps & shampoos
  • 🪵 Wood is termite-resistant
  • 📖 Mentioned in ancient Indian texts
  • 👩‍🌾 Helps make organic fertilizers
  • 🦠 Naturally kills bacteria and fungi
  • ⚖️ Balances the ecosystem 🌱
  • 📚 Encourages learning about nature & healing
